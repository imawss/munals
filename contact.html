Q&A

What languages will be used during the event?
The event will have committees in Turkish, English, and German.
Will shuttle services be provided for transportation?
Shuttle services will not be provided. However, accommodation can be arranged for participants coming from outside the city for an additional fee.
Will food and beverages be provided during the event?
All participants will be provided with food, desserts, water, and print materials during the event.
